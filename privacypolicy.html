Privacy Policy

 

As Heyo Chat, we value the privacy and security of our users. Therefore, while we clearly disclose how we use and share the data we collect, we also respect the preferences and choices of our users.

 

Collection and Use of Data

 

As Heyo Chat, we collect certain information to create user profiles, manage messaging, and improve our services. This information may be automatically collected or provided explicitly by users during the use of services provided by us. For example, we collect username, password, and contact information to store user profile information and messaging.

 

Sharing of Data

 

As Heyo Chat, we do not share the data we collect with third parties. However, we may share data with official authorities if there is a legal obligation or if it is necessary to protect the security of our users.

 

Privacy Preferences

 

As Heyo Chat, we respect the privacy preferences of our users and respect their choices. Users can always update or delete the information they share in their profiles. In addition, users have the option to cancel their membership if they do not wish to receive newsletters and other product and service information.

 

Partner Businesses

 

As Heyo Chat, we work with partner businesses to develop and improve our services. These partner businesses have a responsibility to protect the privacy and security of our data while performing tasks such as collecting, processing, and using our data.

 

Updates and Changes

 

As Heyo Chat, we may update and change our privacy policy from time to time. Therefore, we recommend that you check our privacy policy frequently. Changes to our privacy policy will take effect on the date of publication.

 

Questions and Concerns

 

As Heyo Chat, we value the privacy and security of our users and would like you to contact us if you have any concerns. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us.
